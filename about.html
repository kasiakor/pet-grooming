    About Us

    The Ealing Pet Shop and The Grooming Salon is the one stop shop for all your dog’s needs.

    We offer pet supplies as well as comprehensive dog grooming at an affordable price. Our lovely groomers are fully qualified and trained to provide the best pampering experience for your beloved pets.

    We offer a range of services from a simple nail trim or ear cleaning to full bath, full groom, teeth cleaning as well as expressing anal glands.

    We love all animals. Their welfare and well-being is of utmost importance to us. The grooming salon is cage free and your pets can freely play.

    Comfort and well being of our clients.

    Aromatherapy

    Aromatherapy reduces behavioural issues such as anxiety, obsessive behaviour and aggression as well as physical conditions such as skin problems, arthritis and allergies. We use essential oils to support the body in the process of healing and introducing a sense of well being.

    Music

    Did you know that dogs can perceive frequency double that of humans and can hear sounds approximately four times further away than us. Music has a greater effect on dogs than we ever imagined… For our furry customers we choose classical music, soft rock and reggae. This has a calming effect on them and lowers their heart rate.

    Tellington T Touch Method

    T Touch is a gentle form of touch therapy which relaxes muscle tension and allows the heart rate, blood pressure and circulation to slow down in order to bring the stress levels down. T Touch is based on a mindful approach to working with animals.Tellington T Touch for dogs can help with:

    • Excessive barking or chewing
    • Leash pulling
    • Jumping up
    • Aggressive behaviour
    • Extreme fear and shyness
    • Resistance to grooming
    • Excitability and nervousness
    • Problems associated with ageing
